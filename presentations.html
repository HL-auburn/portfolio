My Presentations

2024 Southeastern Writing Center Association (SWCA)

Location: Atlanta, AL
Date and time: February 22-24, 2024

Co-present with Okunola Odenniyi on the topic: Promoting Access and Support through Asynchronous

           

This conference focuses on the theme of "Writing Center Movements," calling on participants to investigate how writing centers and writers are moving, to where, and with whom.

       

Since the 2022 Summer, University Writing at Auburn adopted asynchronous tutoring. This tutoring appointment has been increased each semester according to the data. We've also found the advantages it can bring to diverse populations, such as untraditional, multilingual, and cognitive needs students, etc. Thus, Ola and I decided to share our research with participants in this conference.
